动词


1. 实义动词 (Notional Verbs)
及物动词
transitive verb (vt.)
动词后可直接跟宾语
I love you.
She reads books every day.
不及物动词
intransitive verb (vi.)
动词后不可直接跟宾语
或需要通过介词再接宾语
Tom left.
She looks at me.
2. 系动词 (Linking Verbs)
be动词
是
  • am, is, are...
I am a doctor.是
She is very beautiful.是
感官动词
  • feel
  • taste
  • smell
  • sound
  • look
  • remain
  • stay
  • keep
  • become
  • get
  • turn
  • grow
  • go
The bread tastes good.
The flower smells very sweet.
3. 情态动词 (Modal Verbs)
情态动词基本规则
could/might/would 是 can/may/will 的过去式
过去式语气比原形更加委婉
情态动词无人称变化
情态动词+动词原形
情态动词语气差别

√ 表可能

might < may < could < can < should < must

可能性从小到大排列

√ 表许可

might < may < could < can

礼貌程度递增

can could
表能力（时态差别） 现/将
I can do it.
She could do it when she was five years old.
表可能（时态语气差别）
It can't/couldn't be true.
表请求（语气差别）
Can/Could I use your phone?
may might
表可能 (时态/语气差别)
It may/might be true.
表请求 (语气差别)
May/Might I use your phone?
will would
表意愿 (时态/语气差别)
Will you open the door for me?
Would you like a cup of tea?
should
表应该
You should help her.
表可能
The film should be very good.
must
表必须
You must finish it tonight.
表可能
It must be John's book.
4. 助动词be,do,have (Auxiliary Verbs)
助动词的主要功能
构成时态
构成语态
构成疑问和否定
1. 构成时态
I am doing my homework now.
（现在进行时）
I have finished my homework.
（现在完成时）
I will do my homework tomorrow.
（一般将来时）
2. 构成语态
主动
They built the house in 1998.
被动
The house was built in 1998.

（被动语态中的was用红色高亮标出）

3. 构成疑问句、否定句
陈述句： I love you.
否定句： I don't love you.
疑问句： Do you love me?
